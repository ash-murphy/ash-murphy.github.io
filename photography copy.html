    HOW IT ALL BEGAN

    I’ve spent my summers volunteering and teaching special needs children in Inida, Ghana, Vietnam, Cambodia and Uganda. I am in awe of how people who materially have so little can be so generous. This concept inspired the subject of my thesis investigating the question are third world children exploited in charity Ad campaigns. During my travels I kept a photo diary here are a few!
