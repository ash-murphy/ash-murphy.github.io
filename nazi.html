    TRAIL OF TERROR

    Book Design

    Is History repeating itself? Are Isis the new
    nazis? These are the questions I ask in this book.
    The book is split into three sections the Nazis. The similarities between the Nazis and Isis. The last section is about Isis. I wanted the visual language to be harsh and intense in order to represent the Nazi and Isis trail of terror. Each section has a slightly different visual language. The first section is the boldest. The second, is fragmented almost like an unmade jigsaw, putting the pieces of past and present together. The final section is tame letting the reader form their own opinion on the topic.
