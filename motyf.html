    TRANSFORMING TYPOGRAPHY

    Identity Design

    MOTYF is a typography festival that will be held in Dublin in 2019. We were asked the question, what is the future of typography? I represented the past and future of typography through photography. I photographed letters peeling off the page, as if they are getting up and moving toward the future. The shadow of the peeling letters are perfectly formed – representing the past of typography. While the peeling abstract forms represent the future and the mystery that is to come.
