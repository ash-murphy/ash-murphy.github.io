    BANISHED

    Opera Identity

    Banished is an Opera about women being banished from Ireland and transported as criminals on a ship to Australia. This journey was tough and grim. The poster represents a girl whose life is so grim that her only release is when she closes her eyes and sees colour in her dreams. I wanted to take the positive out of the negative and show colour in a very black and white world. The booklet represents the sense of motion through the oddly shaped typography. The cut outs reveal two levels representing the segregation of the ship. I contrasted textures I found in Kilmainham Gaol representing her current life with the colours of her dreams representing potential and hope.
