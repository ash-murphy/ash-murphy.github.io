    MALDIVES

    Movie Titles

    I created an opening title sequence for the short film Maldives. This is a film with many unnerving themes such as fear, anxiety, sexuality and confusion. The film is based on the directors seven dreams. I wanted to capture the atmosphere of these dreams through a frantic visual language. The white hand represents the lack of control you have over your dreams. The hand rendered type is to mimic how you scribble in your diary so you don't forget your thoughts.
